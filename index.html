Who am I?

I'm Umaimah, a frontend developer who loves turning ideas into real, interactive, and beautiful websites.

With a strong focus on clean code, responsive design, and modern UI trends, I build digital experiences that are both elegant and functional.

It all began with a schoolgirl’s curiosity — wondering how websites appeared on her mobile screen. That simple question sparked something deep, and over time, that curiosity turned into passion. What started as fascination soon became purpose. Today, I build the kind of websites I used to be amazed by — thoughtful, responsive, and crafted with care.
